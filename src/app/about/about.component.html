About Me

One of the last barber stylists

Barber stylists who specialize in cutting men's hair the right way is a dying breed. A barber knows how a man's hair needs to be cut!

Having been a barber for over 47 years, I've honed my skill to an art. I guarantee you'll love the haircut you'll receive.

In the day, as a senior at BYU, I was drafted into the US army. Upon returning from the army and to take care of my family, I attended and graduated from Salt Lake Barber college where I found great success in cutting hair. After working with another barber for a short time, I bought out the business and formed a business of my own, Evan's Roman Way, in Orem Utah.

I specialize in men's hair cutting, straight edge razor hair cutting, and beard trimming.

I handle hair with care and use the right tools for the right job. Whether your hair is curley, wavey, straight, long, short, thick, or thin, I can cut your hair right and make you look good. I can cut your hair to the style you like and give you suggestions of styles that would work best for your hair type.
